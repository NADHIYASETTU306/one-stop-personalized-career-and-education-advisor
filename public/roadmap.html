Career Development Roadmap

Degree → Skills → Internships → Jobs / Higher Studies

Year 1 - Foundations 🚀

Skills: Python, C/C++, DSA, Math Basics

Courses: Programming, DBMS Basics, Calculus

Internships/Projects: Mini coding projects, GitHub

Goals: Build strong foundation & maintain good CGPA

Year 2 - Core Data Science 🔍

Skills: Data Analysis, SQL, ML Basics

Courses: Statistics, Data Science

Internships/Projects: Kaggle challenges, small ML projects

Goals: Strengthen portfolio & GPA

Year 3 - Advanced AI 🤖

Skills: Deep Learning, NLP, Computer Vision

Courses: AI, Big Data, Cloud Basics

Internships/Projects: Research, summer internship

Goals: Publish project/research paper

Year 4 - Industry Readiness 🌐

Skills: MLOps, Cloud Platforms, AI Ethics

Courses: Capstone Project, Interview Prep

Internships/Projects: Full-time internship, freelancing

Goals: Apply for Jobs / Higher Studies
